ABOUT THE FIRM

As a multifaceted firm, we have been operating for close to a decade now. It all began in the year 2008 when I started with filling of application forms and processing visa applications for people to the U.S embassy in the Gambia. The saying that a journey of thousand miles begin with a step was at play but I did not realize it till of late. As I look at how far I’ve come, I really see that yes it just took a step to get to where I am today.

The implementation of e-visa application by the U.S embassy was what brought about my preeminence in the business as at then. I was called upon by people from all works of life for my services.

After I buckled up and made up my mind to focus on application form filling and visa processing, I decided to expand my territory to other countries too: I plunged into processing European countries too most especially the Schengen.

As at now, my specialty in study abroad program is focused on America and CANADA. We are on top of the list when it comes to CANADIAN and USA visa application process.

SERVICES

VISA APPLICATION

As at now throughout the Gambia, we are the only firm that does and specializes in CANADIAN visa processing. Individuals from all works of life – from private to government personnel, all come to us for counselling and application of their CANADIAN visa. Though CANADA and USA is our focal point as at now, we still render services for all other countries. We process: Student visas, visitor visas, permanent resident visas, etc.

COUNSELLING

Our office is flooded with counselees 24/7.From the young to the elderly and sometimes parents with their children on how to tackle their Studies, business and many other issues. Some that decided to stay have also excelled in businesses.

About Me

The Founder of the above name Company is the able Foday Jawara Native of Barajally Tenda Niani District Central River Region, In the Republic of The Gambia. He started His Educational Career at the Barajally’s Primary School in 1995 and later proceed to Bansang Junior Secondary School And finally completed His High School In Gambia Senior Secondary School Banjul in 2005. After High School He was Admitted to University of the Gambia.
